In honour of the world's greatest fruit, Pineapple Friday is an annual holiday that falls in late November. The website was designed to provide dates, details, and locations for anyone interested in participating in the celebration. It was built using ZURB Foundation and typefaces from Google Web Fonts and The League of Moveable Type.
