What is Yoga Life Coaching?

Yoga Life Coaching is, essentially, a conversation. It is both powerful and purposeful. It assists clients in getting clear with what they want, reaches something deep inside themselves, and provides a supportive environment for clients to take both responsibility and action in their lives in order to be their best self.

What is Yoga Life Coaching with Charli?

Do you feel stuck or in a rut?

Do you want to live a passionate and empowered life?

Do you enjoy self-work?

If you answered yes to any or all of the above, yoga life coaching can benefit you!

I offer 30 minute coaching sessions via phone call where I create a safe and supportive environment for you to let your guard down, open up, and get to something within you. I will give you my full attention. I will listen intently. I will ask simple but powerful questions. I will give you a firm but gentle nudge towards finding your own answers, clarity, and desires. I will hold you responsible for your thoughts, feelings, and actions. I will help you recognize your true and beautiful self. I will help you recognize what you have to offer this world. I will guide you to take steps towards your dreams. I will offer homework when I see it will benefit you. The purpose of the homework is to get you moving towards the bright and shiny life that is right in front of you.
